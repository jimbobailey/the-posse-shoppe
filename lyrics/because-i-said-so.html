Because I Said So

Spoken Intro This is for the ones who grew up drinking from the garden hose. The ones who left at sunrise and didn't come back 'til the streetlights flickered on. We didn't have a safety net, and we didn't have a say. "Because I said so" was the law of the land... and respect wasn't something you negotiated. It was a different world back then... but it made us who we are today.

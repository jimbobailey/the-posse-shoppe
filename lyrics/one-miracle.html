Who Told You That

[Verse 1] If I had a choice of one prayer to come true, It would be the prayer I pray for you. I'd ask Him to calm the storm inside, And let God's peace become your guide. Force every anxious thought to cease, And bring your restless mind to ease. To break the grip that's held you bound, And place your feet on solid ground. [Chorus] If Jesus gave me one miracle, One blessing He'd let me choose, I wouldn't keep it for myself, I'd give it all to you. To calm the storm behind your eyes, And erase the pain you've been through, If Jesus gave me one miracle, I'd spend it all on you. [Verse 2] I've watched you fight battles no one can see, Carrying a weight that was never meant to be. I've tried it a father's way, doing all I knew, Some storms are too mighty for a father to subdue. So I placed your name in the Savior's hands, Trusting His heart when I can't understand. And though I can't carry this burden for you, I'll stand beside you until He sees you through. [Chorus] If Jesus gave me one miracle, One blessing He'd let me choose, I wouldn't keep it for myself, I'd give it all to you. To calm the storm behind your eyes, And erase the pain you've been through, If Jesus gave me one miracle, I'd spend it all on you. [Verse 3] Healing is coming, I believe that to be true, The same God who carried me will carry you. Lay all your worries at the foot of the cross, He can restore all that was lost. Hold to His promise when you can't see the way, Trusting He's working even now as you pray. And when peace finally rises where anxiety stood, You'll see He was doing what only He could. [Bridge] If Daddy could've fixed it, it would've been done, I'd have fought your battles, every single one. I'd have taken your pain and carried it too, There's nothing Daddy wouldn't have done for you. This mountain is higher than these hands can climb, So I'm sending it to my Savior one more time. And I'll keep believing, trusting His plan, 'Cause Jesus can do what no father can. [Final Chorus] If Jesus gave me one miracle, One blessing He'd let me choose, I wouldn't keep it for myself, I'd give it all to you. To calm the storm behind your eyes, And erase the pain you've been through, If Jesus gave me one miracle, I'd spend it all on you. "If there's one thing I hope you always knew...

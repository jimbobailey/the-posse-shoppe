About the Shop

I run a small shop from my home focusing on two things: 3D Printing and Laser Engraving. Everything you see here is made by me.

3D Printing

The items in the shop are just the start. If you have a specific part or a project you need printed that isn't listed here, feel free to reach out. I can’t promise I can do every single job, but send me a message with what you’re looking for and I’ll let you know if it’s something I can handle.

Laser Engraving

I use two different types of lasers depending on the job.

Wood, Acrylic, Leather, and Leatherette: These are done on my CO2 laser. This machine can handle larger projects, with a working area up to 40" x 63".

Metal: For metal items, I use a Fiber laser with an 11" x 11" capacity.

Popular Custom Work

One of my most popular items is custom Trucker Caps. I engrave the designs onto adhesive-backed leatherette and apply them to the hats myself using a heat press. If you’d rather do the assembly yourself, I can also sell you just the patches.

Whether it's a gift or something for your own use, I’m open to new ideas. If you’ve got a concept for an engraving or a custom patch, hit me up and we’ll see if it’ll work.
